What is a fidget cube?
The Fidget Cube is a small handheld device (known as a fidget toy) designed by Antsy Labs. It has sensory tools on all sides: an on/off-style switch, gears, a rolling ball, a small joystick, a spinning disc, a "rubbing stone", and 5 buttons. The cube is intended to provide an easy way to occupy one's hands and other senses, particularly for self-soothing.
